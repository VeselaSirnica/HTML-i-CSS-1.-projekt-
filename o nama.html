Why was there molten metal at 9/11 Ground Zero?

Neither steel nor any other metal melted on 9/11. There is no EVIDENCE (videos or photos) of molten metal (that is liquid metal). Yes, there were accounts of metal/steel “flowing like lava” and videos show what seemed ignited material/sparks coming out of WTC 2 just before its destruction. However, especially witness testimony/accounts and video (much better of course) are the weakest kind of EVIDENCE.

Physical EVIDENCE is the best and most trustworthy testimony of WHAT happened on 9/11. It is impossible that anything, let alone concrete or metal, was “burning” under the debris for months. Yes, it is true there are photos of glowing metal beams/pipes/cladding (steel and aluminum), but not liquid metal, just glowing metal. However, everything that’s hot glows (over 600°C more or less), but not everything that glows is hot. Take for example a fluorescent light. Also rapid rusting of metal was observed on some cars and steel beams/pipes. Why? Some other physical process not previously encountered was at work on 9/11. The molten metal story is just a myth. For example consider the reports of the workers steel-toed boots melting (no video EVIDENCE unfortunately).

I’m not say they lied, I’m just saying that some other physical process must be introduced that can give the molten and glowing appearance to metal. After all there were no reports of burnt or, should we say, molten feet! If the steel in the boots melted, how could the feet not be more than cooked!? How could the workers and rescue teams walk on the alleged hot rubble (supposedly heated by the fires/molten metal under the debris) without being grilled like on a BBQ? Consider all the water they poured on the rubble.

What looked like steam rising from the debris, due to water turning to steam because of the alleged heat, was in fact dust. The building remains, mainly steel and concrete, have suffered some kind of molecular dissociation and continued tot do so for weeks/months. During the 1st year’s rembrance cerimony in September 2002 the ground was still fuming with dust.
